He was a witness on 2 Jan. 149% to the will of his wife's brother, Henry Vere of Addington, esquire, sheriff of Northants in 1485.12

His wife was Ellen Vere, daughter of Richard Vere of Addington and Thrapston, Northants (who died in 1480) by Isabel, daughter of John Green of Drayton. She was probably married in 1485, for by a deed of 11 May 1485 the feoffees of William Isham's Northamptonshire lands gave them to his son Thomas Isham and Ellen his wife and the heirs of their bodies.
